OUR WORK

WE PROVIDE THE SOLUTION.

Hydraulic cylinders work under extreme pressure. Sooner or later the continuous raising, lowering, shifting will cause wear which can endanger the entire system. A2 Engineering services are not just much cheaper than buying a new hydraulic cylinder, but it also guarantees the full functionality of the overhauled component in an equivalent manner to a new cylinder, and it does so within a very short time. This means downtimes can be minimised and significant investment sums saved.

HOW THE REPAIR PROCESS WORKS:

  • 1. Get in touch to discuss your requirements. We’ll discuss your situation, make recommendations, and ensure that we’re able to repair the unit in question.
  • 2. Bring us your unit — and specify that you are needing a quote for a repair.
  • 3. We will provide a complimentary evaluation to determine the source of the problem.
  • 4. We will keep you informed of our findings and alert you as to the cost of the repair — assuming it’s one we can make. We will also provide a timeframe for when it will be completed.
  • 5. Once you give us your approval, we will begin work!
