ABOUT US

A2 ENGINEERING IS THE COST-EFFECTIVE ALTERNATIVE TO EXPENSIVE NEW INVESTMENT.

We are specialised in hydraulic cylinder services. We serve customers across industries, bringing relentless innovation and quality to every product we make. Our reputation is built on many years of experience and everything we do is geared towards powering the future.

OUR WORK

WE PROVIDE THE SOLUTION.

Hydraulic cylinders work under extreme pressure. Sooner or later the continuous raising, lowering, shifting will cause wear which can endanger the entire system. A2 Engineering services are not just much cheaper than buying a new hydraulic cylinder, but it also guarantees the full functionality of the overhauled component in an equivalent manner to a new cylinder, and it does so within a very short time. This means downtimes can be minimised and significant investment sums saved.

OUR SERVICES

DESIGN, MANUFACTURE AND REPAIR WITH THE HIGHEST QUALITY.

Efficient solution

The state-of-art technology deployed in the repair service is able to meet any challenge. Even major wear and partial erosion can be repaired - whether on a common model or a bespoke cylinder type. The range of services offered covers the full spectrum, from replacement of seals of all kind to repair or replacement of individual components.

Metalworking

A2 Engineering supports its customers even beyond cylinder repair. Modern CNC machinery is also available to take on last minute orders including lathe, welding as well as milling work.

Cost transparency

Prior to carrying out any job, a quotation is drawn up specifying all the necessary work to achieve a complete, professional repair of the defective hydraulic cylinder. This cost transparency means unpleasant surprises in downstream phases are avoided.
